Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use these trading signals?

+

Our trading signals are designed to be easy to follow. Each signal includes detailed entry price, stop loss, and take profit levels. Simply enter the trade at the specified entry price, set your stop loss and take profit as indicated, and monitor the signal status for updates.

What is the success rate of your signals?

+

Our signals have maintained a consistent success rate of 75-80% over the past year. However, we always recommend using proper risk management practices as the market conditions can change.

How are the signals delivered?

+

Signals are delivered in real-time through our website dashboard, email notifications, and our Telegram channel. Pro and VIP members receive instant notifications when new signals are posted.

Can I cancel my subscription?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

What markets do you provide signals for?

+

We provide signals for Forex major and minor pairs, cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major altcoins), commodities like Gold and Silver, and major stock indices.

Do you provide trading education?

+

Yes, all our members get access to basic trading education materials. VIP members receive additional advanced training resources and one-on-one strategy calls with our expert traders.
